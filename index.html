Social Impact

Every year, 1.3 billion tons of food is lost or wasted. We are currently facing a severe food shortage created by the global food system, but the negative effects of wasteful buying extend to areas above that, contributing to climate change, deforestation, pollution, loss of soil, loss of biodiversity, and more. [1]

About two-thirds of food waste at home is due to food not being used before it goes bad. Food spoilage at home occurs due to improper storage, lack of visibility in refrigerators, partially used ingredients and misjudged food needs. [2]

We created Plater to help reduce food waste by allowing you to make the delicious recipes out of all the food ingredients you bought home. We remind you and give you related recipes when an ingredient is about to expire so you will save as much as a thousand dollars [3] on groceries a year and never contribute to food waste again!
